About Us

Discover the story behind RICH KAPEHAN.

In Blendington, Richo Brewster transformed a family coffee shop into RICH Kapehan, an exclusive haven for coffee enthusiasts. With opulent decor and exotic blends, it became a social hotspot, attracting elites. The brand expanded, symbolizing luxury and leaving a lasting mark on coffee culture.
